Live! for YouTube

     

“Live! for YouTube” will allow you to live stream on your YouTube channel with your Android phone.

Very simple "point-and-shoot" application. Easy switch between YouTube accounts and channels, intuitive control over video quality and privacy.

Share your life with your friends and enemies.

What you get practically for free (OK, totally for free):
● Real-time live broadcast to YouTube
● Sharing live broadcast with whole world (ask world's permission first, please)
● Choose your video quality - 240p, 360p, 480p, 720p or 1080p (depends on the phone / tablet)
● Choose your audio quality - mono or stereo (depends on the phone / tablet)

Please note: you need to enable live streaming for your YouTube channel in order to use this application. For additional details visit YouTube site: https://www.youtube.com/features
